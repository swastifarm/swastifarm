We are a family-owned farm. We believe that agricultural modernization is essential for fulfilling demands of the rising modern population whose interest fade away for farming.

What We Grow

We cultivate a diverse range of seasonal vegetables and cereals, always prioritizing soil health and biodiversity. From watery cucumbers in summer to crunchy cabbage in winter, and from smooth ghee to viscous honey, our farm provides fresh, delicious produce throughout the year.
